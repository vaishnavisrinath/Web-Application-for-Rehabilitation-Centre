SAY  NO  TO  DRUGS !

VISION

Recently, through media we encountered a great expose of drug mafia practiced in our country. From uneducated to highly educated, from poor to rich, from a common man to a celebrity, most of the people are easily falling into the hands of the drug mafia. The major concern is about our youth and the teenagers who are spoiling their budding careers knowingly or unknowingly by falling for drugs. With all of the pressures in a teen's life, drugs and alcohol may seem like a tempting "escape" from reality.

Our goal is to provide accurate and trustworthy information about drugs and the risks that come with the use of drugs in order to keep teens and other common people informed. This website covers information about various domains related to drugs such as varieties of drugs, laws against drugs, disorders caused by using banned drugs, use of drugs in preparing vaccination for covid-19 pandemic, Doping of drugs in sports, etc. And also makes it easy for the common people to book the de-addiction centers aroud our state.
